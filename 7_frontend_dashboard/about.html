About AquaSafe AI

AquaSafe AI is an intelligent microscopy system designed to automate microorganism detection, plankton identification, and safety assessment. Using advanced Deep Learning, Computer Vision, and embedded hardware, it provides fast, accurate, and real-time analysis of water samples.

Our Mission

To make water-quality monitoring effortless, reliable, and accessible by combining modern AI technology with innovative microscopy solutions. We believe that clean water is essential for all life, and our mission is to provide the tools and technologies that make it easier to monitor and protect water quality worldwide.

What AquaSafe AI Does

  1. Automated Microorganism Detection – Detects microbes and plankton species using trained AI models and deep learning algorithms.
  2. Plankton Counting & Classification – Counts plankton, identifies species, and generates quick insights about ecosystem health.
  3. Real-Time Safety Analysis – Warns about potential contamination or harmful microorganism levels in water samples.
  4. Embedded Microscopy Integration – Works smoothly on low-cost embedded systems and portable hardware setups.
  5. Instant Report Generation – Produces annotated images, visuals, and comprehensive logs for analysis and documentation.

Why AquaSafe AI?

  • Reduces manual microscope work and saves time in water quality monitoring
  • Improves accuracy with AI-powered detection and analysis
  • Faster results than traditional manual observation methods
  • Works in remote or limited-resource environments
  • Helps water-safety agencies and environmental researchers make informed decisions
  • Supports sustainable development and clean water initiatives
