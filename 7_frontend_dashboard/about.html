About AquaSafe AI

AquaSafe AI is an intelligent microscopy system designed to automate microorganism detection, plankton identification, and safety assessment. Using advanced Deep Learning, Computer Vision, and embedded hardware, it provides fast, accurate, and real-time analysis of water samples.

Our Mission

To make water-quality monitoring effortless, reliable, and accessible by combining modern AI technology with innovative microscopy solutions.

What AquaSafe AI Does

  • 1
    Automated Microorganism Detection
    Detects microbes and plankton species using trained AI models.
  • 2
    Plankton Counting & Classification
    Counts plankton, identifies species, and generates quick insights.
  • 3
    Real-Time Safety Analysis
    Warns about potential contamination or harmful microorganism levels.
  • 4
    Embedded Microscopy Integration
    Works smoothly on low-cost embedded systems.
  • 5
    Instant Report Generation
    Produces annotated images, visuals, and logs.

Why AquaSafe AI?

- Reduces manual microscope work
- Improves accuracy with AI
- Faster than traditional observation
- Works in remote or limited environments
- Helps water-safety and environmental research
